# Threat Hunting Basics

## What is Threat Hunting?

Proactively searching for threats that may have bypassed security controls.

## Common Hunting Areas

- PowerShell Activity
- Failed Logins
- New Users
- Suspicious Processes
- Persistence Mechanisms

## Example

User normally logs in at 9 AM.

Today login occurred at 3:17 AM.

This behavior should be investigated.

## SOC Analyst Questions

- Who logged in?
- From where?
- What happened after login?
- Were privileges changed?
